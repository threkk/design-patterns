CONTROLS:
    WASD: Move
    Left Mouse Button or SPACE: Use weapon
    ESCAPE: Quit to Menu
    1: Use Machine Gun
    2: Use Charge Blast
    3: Use Missile Launcher
    F11: Toggle Performance Profiler
    =: Toggle laptop and desktop mode.

LAPTOP MODE
	In case people do not have a satisfactory mouse, Micron allows for "laptop mode", where the player uses the arrow keys to aim.
	
DESKTOP MODE
	The default mode, where the player uses the mouse to aim. This is the recommended gameplay mode.

The bar at the bottom shows your current health. It regenerates over time.

NOTE: At any time, hit (Shift + ESC) to exit the game immediately.
